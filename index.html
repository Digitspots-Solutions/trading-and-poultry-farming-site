About Us

Trading and Poultry Farming is a leading poultry manufacturing business located in the heart of Owerri Municipal. We specialize in breeding, raising, and supplying high-quality chickens, eggs, and poultry products to retailers, restaurants, and households. Our commitment to hygiene, animal welfare, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice for poultry needs in Imo State. With years of experience in the industry, we maintain strict quality control measures to ensure every product that leaves our farm meets the highest standards of freshness and safety.
